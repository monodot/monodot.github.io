# Basic Countdown Timer App: Support & Privacy Policy

## Support information

For support with the app, please contact <a href="https://x.com/monodot">@monodot</a> on X.

## Privacy Policy

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to your personal data in Basic Countdown Timer.

Details of individual timers, and their titles, are stored only on your device and destroyed when the next timer is created.

We do not collect any user information, personally-identifiable or otherwise, from the app.
